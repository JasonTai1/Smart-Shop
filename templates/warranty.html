Warranty Policy

Last updated: May 2026 ✓ Warranty Covered 12 month warranty

How Warranty Works

If your item has a defect, you can submit a warranty claim through your account. Our team will review and decide whether to repair, replace, or reject the claim.

Claims are subject to verification. Not all requests will be approved.

Eligibility

  • Item has manufacturing defect
  • Within warranty period
  • Proof of purchase is provided
  • Item is not damaged by misuse

Not Covered

  • Physical damage caused by user
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Unauthorized modification or repair

Submit a Warranty Claim

Go to your orders and select the product to request warranty.
