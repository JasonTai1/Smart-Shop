📦 Orders & Shipping

How can I track my order? +
Go to My Account → Orders and select the order you want to track. You'll see real-time courier updates and an estimated delivery date.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location.
Can I change my delivery address after placing an order? +
You can edit your delivery address within 1 hour of placing the order, as long as it hasn't been shipped. Go to My Account → Orders → Edit Address.

💳 Payments

What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept Visa, Mastercard, online banking (FPX), e-wallets (Touch 'n Go, GrabPay), and cash on delivery for selected areas.
Why was my payment declined? +
This may be due to incorrect card details, insufficient funds, or your bank blocking the transaction. Try another payment method or contact your bank.

↩️ Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy? +
Items can be returned within 7 calendar days of delivery if unused, undamaged, and in original packaging. See our full Return & Refund Policy for details.
When will I get my refund? +
Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. It may take an extra 2-7 days to appear on your statement.

👤 Account & Security

How do I reset my password? +
Click Forgot Password on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.
How do I update my account details? +
Go to My Account → Profile Settings to update your name, email, phone number, or address.

🎁 Promotions & Membership

How do I become a Premium Member? +
Sign up for SmartShop Premium from your account dashboard to enjoy an extra 20% off on selected items and free shipping.
How do I apply a promo code? +
Enter your promo code at checkout in the "Promo Code" box and click Apply. The discount will be reflected in your order total.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you.
