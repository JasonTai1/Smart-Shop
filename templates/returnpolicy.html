To start a return, go to My Account → Orders → Request Return and select the item you wish to return. Our team will review and approve your request within 1 business day.

📌  All returns must be submitted within 7 calendar days of the delivery date shown in your order confirmation email.

✅ Return eligibility

Items are eligible for a return if they meet all of the following conditions:

  • Returned within 7 calendar days of the delivery date
  • Item is unused, undamaged, and in original condition
  • Original packaging, accessories, and manuals are included
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is provided
  • Item has not been assembled, installed, or activated

❌ Non-returnable items

The following categories are not eligible for returns or exchanges:

  • Opened software, digital downloads, or software licences
  • Personalised or custom-made products
  • Hygiene-sensitive products (earphones, shavers) once unsealed
  • Items damaged due to misuse, accident, or water damage
  • Products purchased during a clearly marked final-sale promotion
  • Consumable items (batteries, ink cartridges) once opened

💰 Refunds

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, your refund will be processed. Refund timelines vary by payment method:

Payment method Refund timeline
Online Banking (FPX) 3–5 business days
Touch 'n Go eWallet 1–3 business days

You will receive an email notification once your refund has been processed. If you have not received your refund after the stated period, please contact your bank or payment provider first, then reach out to us.

🔄 Exchanges

Need a different size, colour, or variant? We offer free exchanges for the same product.

Simply select "Exchange" instead of "Refund" when submitting your return request. Exchanged items will be shipped within 2 business days of us receiving your original item, subject to stock availability.

If the desired variant is out of stock, we will process a full refund instead.

🛡️ Warranty claims

If your product is faulty or defective, it may be covered under the manufacturer's warranty in addition to our return policy. Warranty coverage varies by brand and product category.

To make a warranty claim, please contact us with:

  1. Your order number
  2. A clear photo or video showing the defect
  3. A brief description of the issue

Our team will advise you on the next steps, which may include a repair, replacement, or refund depending on the case.

📞 Contact support

If you have any questions about your return or refund, our support team is here to help.
